GLAZW is the official cybersecurity club of MIST, born from a shared passion for digital defense, ethical hacking, and cyber awareness. We’re a community of curious minds—coders, analysts, and puzzle-solvers—who thrive on protecting systems and decoding vulnerabilities. From beginner-friendly workshops on Linux and cryptography to advanced CTFs and red-blue team simulations, GLAZW empowers students to explore the full spectrum of cybersecurity. Our motto? Learn, Hack, Secure. Beyond technical mastery, GLAZW is a movement to foster cyber hygiene across campus. We host awareness campaigns, guest lectures by industry experts, and collaborative projects that tackle real-world threats—from phishing to data breaches. Whether you're a seasoned hacker or just cyber-curious, GLAZW offers a safe, inclusive space to grow, experiment, and contribute to a digitally secure future. Join us, and let’s make MIST a fortress of cyber resilience.
